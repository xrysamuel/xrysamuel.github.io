College English 4 - Translation

Time: 2022/10/11
Author: XrySamuel
Material: 4759-3-段落翻译

Consideration in doing translation

  • wording (appropriateness / variety, avoid monotony, use the words flexibly)
  • be faithful to the source text semantically and stylistically
  • use the grammar and sentence structures correctly and skillfully
  • make good use of the translation skills (amplification, omission, conversion of word class, readjustment of the sentence order etc.)
  • pay some attention to the cultural differences (do some interpretative translation) / culture transmission

Examples

Example 1

中国的父母往往过于关注孩子的学习，以至于不要他们帮忙做家务。他们对孩子的首要要求就是努力学习，考得好，能上名牌大学。他们相信这是为孩子好，因为在中国这样竞争激烈的社会里，只有好成绩才能保证前途光明。中国父母还认为，如果孩子在社会上取得大的成就，父母就会受到尊重。因此，他们愿意牺牲自己的时间，爱好和兴趣，为孩子提供更好的条件。

Sentence 1

中国的父母 往往过于关注 孩子的学习，以至于 不要他们 帮忙 做家务。

Wording:

tend to do / be prone to doing ? / be apt to do sth. / be apt at / be liable to do / be inclined to do

pay undue / too much excessive attention to sth. / care too much about / be excessively involved in / be excessively concerned about / for / show too much concern for

help sb. with housework / do one's share of housework / be involved in housework / partake housework ( = house chores)

Structure:

adverbial clause of result

  • so that
  • too to
  • to the point where
  • to the point that
  • so much so that

Reference:

Chinese parents tend to invest heavily in their children’s school work so much so that they do not involve their kids in any housework.

Sentence 2

他们对孩子的 首要要求 就是 努力学习，考得好，能 上名牌大学。

Wording:

main requirements / top concerns / top priorities

study hard / work hard at one's study / study painstakingly / industriously / assiduously / diligently / be bent on one's study / apply oneself to one's study / stay focused on one's study / engross oneself in one's study / be absorbed in one's study

do well in the exams / score high in the exams / to be a high-achiever in the exams / perform stunningly in the exams

go to / attend / be accepted by / be enrolled in / be admitted to

prestigious university / renowned university / internationally-acclaimed university / esteemed university / top-ranking university / world-famous university / great university / world-leading university

Structure:

系表结构，动词不定式作表语；平行结构 Parallel structure

Reference:

To them, the top concerns for their kids are to study hard, to earn high grades in exams, and to be accepted by prestigious universities.

Sentence 3

他们相信这是 为孩子好，因为在中国这样 竞争激烈的 社会里，只有 好成绩 才 能 保证前途光明。

Wording:

for the sake of the kids / for the good of the kids / in the interest of the kids / to the benefit of kids / benefit the kids / work in favour of the kids / put the kids in an favourable position

highly competitive / acute competition / keen competition / fierce competition / intense competition / sharp competition / throat-cut competition / tough competition / strong competition

only ... / nothing but / It is ... that (emphatic structure)

ensure / guarantee / make sure that / assure sb. of sth. / a must for sth.

a bright future / a promising future / a good prospect / a bright prospect / a rosy future

Structure:

Object clause：The believe that ... / They are convinced that ...
Subject clause / Impersonal subject pattern: It is their firm belief that ...

Reference:

They believe that such expectations are in the interests of their children, because it is nothing but good grades that can ensure a bright prospect in such a highly competitive Chinese society.

Sentence 4

中国父母还 认为，如果孩子在社会上 取得大的成就，父母就会受到尊重。

Wording:

consider / believe / think / maintain / claim / argue / insist / reckon / suppose / surmise / assume / suspect / assert / hold the view that / in one's opinion / as sb. sees it / as far as sb. be concerned / to the best of one's knowledge / as far as sb. knows / according to one's understanding

make great achievement / earn great achievement / make a great success / score great achievements / make great fulfillments / make commendable achievements / make stunning achievements / make admirable achievements

earn respect / be respected by others / win due respect / be revered by others

Structure:

宾语从句
条件状语从句

Reference:

Also, Chinese parents insist that they will be duly respected only if their children have made commendable achievements in the society.

(Adjustment of sentence order)

Sentence 5

因此，他们 愿意牺牲 自己的时间，爱好和兴趣，为孩子 提供 更好的 条件。

Wording:

as a result, / therefore, / thus, / consequently, / as a consequence, / hence

be willing to do / offer to do / be ready to do / take delight in / derive much entertainment from / would rather do

sacrifice A for B / make sacrifices for sth.

provide sb. with sth. / provide sth. for sb. / offer sb. sth. / offer sth. to sb. / supply sb. with sth. / supply sth. to sb. / furnish sb. with sth.

conditions (环境 v.s. condition 状态)

Reference:

As a result, for the kids' sake they would rather sacrifice the time, hobbies and interest of their own for the better living conditions.

Example 2

公元前551年，在今天的山东省曲阜诞生了中国古代著名的思想家、教育家孔子。孔子的家里十分贫穷，但是他从小就认真读书，刻苦学习。20多岁的时候做了个小官。他很有学问，办事认真，工作出色，30岁左右就已经很有名了。孔子到过许多诸侯国家，想为各国君王效力，但是他们都不接受他的政治主张。于是孔子决心用自己全部的精力来办学。很多人知道这消息之后，都把自己的孩子送来拜孔子为师。孔子收下了这些学生，开创了中国教育史上的私人讲学事业。

公元前551年，在今天的山东省曲阜诞生了中国古代著名的思想家、教育家孔子。

Translation skills: S + V + O (key structure) 先翻主谓宾，后加定状补

Proper name: 孔子 - Confucius

(In the year of 551 B.C.,) Confucius (, the famous / eminent / renowned / noted / celebrated thinker and educator in ancient China) was born in (today's Qufu of Shandong Province)

Reference:

In the year 551 B.C., Confucius, the famous thinker and educator of ancient China, was born in today’s Qufu, Shandong Province. His family was very poor, but he worked hard at his studies even in early childhood. He became a petty official in his early twenties. As he had great learning, worked in earnest and was outstanding in performing his duties, he had already earned a high reputation by the age of thirty. Confucius visited many principalities in the hope of rendering service to the princes, but none of them would accept his political views. Consequently, he changed his mind and decided to devote all his energies to education. When people learned about this, many of them sent their children to him to be educated. Confucius accepted them as his students, and thus he became the first individual to initiate a private school in the history of Chinese education.
